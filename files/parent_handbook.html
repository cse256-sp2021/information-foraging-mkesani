Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How does Washington University Nursery School communicate with parents?

We make communicating with parents consistently and effectively a priority here at WUNS! Please see page 8 of the Parent Handbook for more details.

Q: How does Washington University Nursery School help make new students’ transition to school as smooth as possible?

For many of our students, coming to our school will be their first academic experience, and we are acutely aware of how difficult this transition can be for both parent and child. We designed our orientation day with this in mind - for more details, please refer to page 8 of the Parent Handbook.

Q: What is Washington University Nursery School’s philosophy on and approach to discipline?

Here at WUNS, we aim to create a positive, nurturing environment for students, and an important part of this is maintaining order in the classroom. To learn more about the specific techniques our teachers use to achieve this, please refer to page 9 of the Parent Handbook.

Q: I have another question about WUNS' preschool philosophy, staff, curriculum, classrooms, events, policies and procedures that hasn't been answered yet.

Our Parent Handbook is a fantastic resource for learning more about our school, as well as the rest of this site. If you still can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us by email or phone.
