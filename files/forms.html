Application Procedure

1. Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

2. An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school, along with a completed Application for Admission (this can be filled out online or printed and submitted as a PDF).

3. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date.

4. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one monthâ€™s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

5. Complete and return the required forms prior to your child's first day of class. The enrollment form must be completed every year that your child attends WUNS, while the developmental history form, child medical examination report form, and immunization consent/history form only need to be completed once.
